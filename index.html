ConstructIT/SiteCraft - Name TBD

ConstructIT is an App that started from an idea to help contrctors and general labourers with making calcuations on the fly. Instead of wasting time, searching for the right formula to calculate measurments on the job, contractors can login to the app and find everything they are looking for in one place.

The app is meant to achieve the following:

  • Save users time on the job by simplifying the calculation process and cost estimation for projects.
  • Help users store calcualtions made on the job so that they can be revisited in the future.
  • Share calcuations with vendors, clients and other third parties.
  • Make complicated calculations simple.
  • More features to come.
